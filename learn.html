Sprint

Sprint triathlons grow in popularity each year. The sprint tends to be a fun distance for first-time triathletes, and it can also be a great primer for more experienced athletes

Olympic

The Olympic triathlon was declared an official triathlon distance of the Sydney Olympic Games in 2000. It’s a competitive endurance sport that’s designed spectator-friendly; usually, the course loops around to accommodate the audience.

Button

Ironman Distance

The Half Ironman triathlon is almost as challenging as the full – but its less rigorous stats lend the race a more encouraging feeling of “I can do this.”

Half-Ironman Distance

The Ironman Triathlon is a grueling distance race populated by persistent athletes aiming simply to survive to the finish line. Okay, that’s a bit dramatic – but it’s an intense race.

History
